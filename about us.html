About Central Zoo

Get to know about Central zoo

Welcome to Central Zoo, a premier destination for wildlife enthusiasts, nature lovers, and conservationists. Our zoo is dedicated to preserving biodiversity, educating visitors about the wonders of the animal kingdom, and providing a safe, natural habitat for a wide range of species.

Central zoo is one of the biggest zoo in the town. The land area of the zoo is 33 acres. Central zoo was established in the year 1980 with 10 acre land but lately it was modified and land area was also added in the year 2000. Proper residing space for all the type of animals has been made and maintains human security at high rate. Proper supply of food for all the animals is maintained and checked at regular intervals.

Help us to protect wildlife around the world.

Our board of trustees oversees the zoo's strategic vision and management, ensuring the best care for wildlife and visitors.
