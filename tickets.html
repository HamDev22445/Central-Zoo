Frequently Asked Questions

  • Discounts on Food & Gift Shops ‚Äì Enjoy special discounts at zoo cafes and souvenir shops.

For membership details, visit the zoo administration office or check our website!
Is there a play area for children?
Yes, the zoo has a dedicated play area with swings, slides, and fun activities for kids. The play zone is designed for children under 12 years to enjoy in a safe environment. Parents can relax nearby while their kids play.
Is photography allowed in the zoo?
Yes, visitors can take pictures with mobile phones and DSLRs for personal use. However, professional photography and videography require prior approval from the administration. Flash photography near animals is strictly prohibited for their safety.
Do you offer special packages for school trips?
Yes, we offer discounted group packages and guided tours for school and educational trips. Our team provides informative sessions to help students learn about wildlife conservation. Schools must book in advance to avail of these special packages.
Is the zoo wheelchair-friendly?
Yes, we have wheelchair-accessible pathways, ramps, and special seating areas for differently-abled visitors. Free wheelchair rental is available at the entrance upon request. Our staff is always available to assist visitors who need extra support.
Are food stalls available inside the zoo?
Yes, multiple food stalls offer snacks, drinks, and meals at affordable prices. Visitors can bring their own food, but plastic bags and outside drinks are not allowed. There are also picnic spots for families to enjoy their meals.
Is there a safari ride in the zoo?
Yes, our exclusive safari ride allows visitors to see lions, cheetahs, and other wild animals in a natural habitat. The safari is conducted in an open jeep with guided commentary for an immersive experience. Advance booking is required for this thrilling adventure.
Is a ticket required to enter the zoo?
Yes, tickets are required for all visitors except children under 5 years. Prices are Rs. 500 for adults, Rs. 300 for children (5-12 years), and free for kids below 5. Special discounts are available for students and senior citizens.
